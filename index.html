About the Game

The dwarfs had a problem. Their ancient tower had been overrun by goblins - filthy, relentless, and very much unwilling to leave. The solution? Hire a wizard.

The Wizard's Tower is a low-poly 3D fantasy action-RPG dungeon crawler. You play as the wizard - contracted by the dwarfs to reclaim their tower floor by floor. What begins as a simple extermination job quickly reveals something far darker lurking in the upper chambers.

Battle through goblin hordes, skeleton warriors, and worse as you ascend. Discover powerful gear, master arcane combat, and uncover the truth behind why the goblins came in the first place. Built in a warm, stylised low-polygon art style crafted by a tiny indie team with enormous ambition.

Gameplay Features

  • Deep Combat System - Block, dodge, and strike your way through goblin hordes and undead enemies with fluid, satisfying combat.
  • Gear Progression - Discover and equip increasingly powerful armour and weapons as you climb each floor.
  • Tower Exploration - Every floor is a new environment with unique hazards, enemies, and secrets waiting to be uncovered.
  • Arcane Narrative - Unravel why the goblins took the tower and what truly lurks at its peak.
  • Solo Indie Passion Project - Built with love, updated regularly, shaped by the community.

A World That Breathes

Every creature in the tower lives by its own rules.

The Wizard's Tower is a living ecosystem. Every goblin, skeleton, and dungeon dweller runs a full AI state machine with dozens of distinct behaviours. They patrol the corridors, fish in underground pools, cook their meals, pray to forgotten gods, laugh with their friends - then snap to alert the moment they sense you. Interrupt their day and they'll hunt you down or flee in terror. Leave them alone long enough and they'll return to sleep, relax, and eventually forget you were ever there.
